Milford Sound, New Zealand

Nature's masterpiece in Fiordland National Park

Why Milford Sound Is Special To Me

Milford Sound is special to me because of its breathtaking natural beauty. The towering cliffs, waterfalls, and serene waters create a peaceful, almost magical atmosphere. It’s a place where I can truly connect with nature and feel awestruck by its grandeur.

How to Get There

Airports:

  • Auckland Airport (AKL): The main international airport, about 30 minutes from the city center.
  • Queenstown Airport (ZQN): For travelers heading to the South Island, about 20 minutes from Queenstown.

Public Transportation:

  • Bus: New Zealand’s bus network is affordable, though not always as fast as renting a car.
  • Taxis & Ride-Sharing: Taxis are available in major cities, and ride-sharing services like Uber operate in places like Auckland and Wellington.

Other places to visit in New Zealand

Mitre Peak

The iconic mountain rising dramatically 5,551 feet from the waters of Milford Sound.

Stirling Falls

A 495-foot waterfall plunging directly into the sound, where boats can approach close enough to feel the spray.

Wellington

Wellington is the capital of New Zealand, known for its vibrant arts scene, beautiful waterfront, and strong coffee culture.

Hobbiton

Often called the "finest walk in the world," this 33-mile trail leads through mountains, rainforests, and valleys to Milford Sound.
